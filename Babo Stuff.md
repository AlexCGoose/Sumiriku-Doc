When they do, each time a creature targets Avram with an attack, Avram rolls a d20 to determine whether the attack instead hits one of the swarms. If he has three swarms around him he must roll a 6 or higher to change the attack's target to a swarm. With two swarms he must roll an 8 or higher, and with one swarm he must roll an 11 or higher. The swarms remain for 1 hour, until Avram goes unconscious, or until he dismisses them as a bonus action.

***Parhelion Blades (Recharge 6).*** Lyra surrounds herself with blades of weave and light, before emanating them out in all directions. All other creatures within 30 feet must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 36 (8d8) radiant damage and is blinded. On a successful save they take half as much damage and are not blinded. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of their turns, ending the effect on a success. After being affected by the blinded condition a creature cannot be blinded this way for the next 24 hours.

### Reactions

A critical hit from a spell attack cannot be negated in this way. For area of effect spells, she must use her reaction before attempting a saving throw or taking damage.

## Ferrault, the Vein

*Medium Humanoid (Elf), Lawful Neutral*
___
**Armor Class** :: 17
**Hit Points**  :: 165 (15d12+60)
**Speed**       :: 35ft.
___
|  STR  |  DEX  |  CON  |  INT  |  WIS  |  CHA  |
|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|:-----:|
|12 (+1)|20 (+5)|18 (+4)|16 (+3)|14 (+2)|10 (+0)|
___
**Saving Throws**        :: Str +4, Dex +8, Con +12, Int +6, Wis +5, Cha +8
**Skills**               :: Survival +?, ?
**Damage Resistances**   :: necrotic
**Proficiency Bonus**    :: +5 (Level 15)
___
***Fey Ancestry.*** Ferrault has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put Ferrault to sleep.
:
***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, Ferrault has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.
:
***Hemocraft.*** Ferrault has a d8 Hemocraft Die, that he uses for specific abilities. If a feature requires him to make a Vital Sacrifice, Ferrault rolls the Hemocraft Die and reduces his current hit points by that amount.
:
***Hunter's Bane.*** Ferrault has advantage on Nature, Religion, and Survival checks related to fey, fiends, or undead.
:
***Blade of Blood and Iron.*** Ferrault has with his bloodstream a sentient living weapon called Hematomania. Hematomania is a +2 rapier. If a creature is hit by this weapon, they must also expend two of their hit dice, roll them, and take the result as necrotic damage. Ferrault heals an amount equal to the necrotic damage dealt. Ferrault can conjure the blade by making a Vital Sacrifice as a Bonus Action.

The blade has a sentience, with an Intelligence of 5, a Wisdom of 5, and a Charisma of 17. It communicates by transmitting emotions to Ferrault, and is possesses a thirst for blood; if no creature has taken damage from rolling their hit die in the previous round, the blade ceases to be magical until Ferrault gives it another Vital Sacrifice as a Bonus Action.
:
***Conjuration and Augmentation.*** Ferrault adds his Constitution modifier to damage from spells and Blood Curses. He also adds his Intelligence modifier to all saving throws.
:
***Legendary Resistance (2/Day).*** If Ferrault fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** Ferrault makes two attacks, either with Hematomania or with his Rapier.
:
***Hematomania.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (1d8 + 9) slashing damage. The target must expend two of their hit dice, roll them, and take the result as necrotic damage. If they have less than two hit die they roll all that are remaining. Ferrault heals an amount equal to the necrotic damage dealt.
:
***Rapier.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (1d8 + 7) slashing damage.
:
***Blood Bolt.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +8 to hit, range 45 ft., one target. *Hit:* 16 (3d6 + 4) necrotic damage, and the target has their movement reduced by 10 feet until the end of their next turn.

### Bonus Actions

***Unsheathe.*** Ferrault makes a Vital Offering and reaches to an open wound on his body and draws Hematomania from it.
:
***Crimson Flame.*** Ferrault makes a Vital Offering and sets his rapier ablaze. It becomes magical, and deals an additional d8 fire damage.

### Curses

Ferrault knows a number of Blood Curses. He can use curses four times per rest, and can make a Vital Offering to amplified the curses.
:
***Curse of Blindness.*** Whenever a creature Ferrault can see within 30 feet makes an attack, he can use his reaction to reduce the result of the attack by a d8. When amplified, all the creature's attack rolls are reduced by 1d8 until the start of Ferrault's next turn.
:
***Curse of Confusion.*** As a bonus action Ferrault can curse a creature he can see within 30 feet that is concentrating. It must subtract a d8 from every Constitution saving throw it makes before the start of your next turn. When amplified, the target mus subtract a d8 from every Constitution saving throw that it makes to maintain its concentration on its current spell.
:
***Curse of Marking.*** As a bonus action Ferrault can curse a creature, causing all of his attacks against the target to deal an additional d8 damage of either fire or necrotic. When amplified, all of his attacks against the target this turn have advantage.
:
***Curse of Siphoning.*** As an action Ferrault can force a creature he can see within 30 feet to make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure the target takes 2d8+3 necrotic damage, and Ferrault gains temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt. If amplified, the creature takes the maximum possible damage.
:
***Curse of Binding.*** As a bonus action Ferrault can force a creature he can see within 30 feet to make a Strength saving throw. On a failure the cursed creature's speed is reduced to 0 and it can't use reactions until the end of his next turn. If amplified, the curse instead lasts for 1 minute. The cursed creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns.

### Legendary Actions

Ferrault can take 2 legendary actions.
:
***Move.*** Ferrault moves up to his speed.
:
***Weapon Attack.*** Ferrault makes a single weapon attack.
:
***Blood Bolt (Costs 2 Actions).*** Ferrault throws a Blood Bolt.

2nd level (3 slots): hold person, invisibility, misty step

3rd level (3 slots): animate dead, counterspell, fireball

### Bonus Actions

***Extract Essence.*** The blood mage points to a corpse of a creature that died within the previous minute, and draws an orb of their blood out of them. The blood mage gains a mote of blood. Each mote counts as two sorcery points for the purpose of using sanguine metamagic. Blood motes can also be used to cast a spells without expending spell slots; each mote used counts for two levels of spell slot.
