***Glissando.*** Remion can fly on his insectoid wings. If he is in the air at the end of this movement, he falls if nothing is holding him aloft.
:
***Legendary Resistance (2/Day).*** If Remion fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
:
***The Show Must Go On.*** When Remion reaches 0 hit points, if any of his performers are still alive, he retreats into a cocoon suspended from the ceiling instead of dying or falling unconscious. While in this cocoon he becomes immune to all damage and his speed becomes 0, but he can act as normal. Once all of his performers have been killed, Remion leaves his cocoon and concludes the show.
:
***Rapturous Applause.*** If Remion is brought to 0 hit points without any of this performers, he concludes the show. ### Reactions

***Trite and Uninspired.*** *Trigger:* A creature Remion can hear attempts to grant another creature a Bardic Inspiration die. *Response---Wisdom Saving Throw:* DC ??, the creature that triggered the Reaction. *Failure:* ? (?d?) Psychic damage and the Bardic Inspiration die is lost.

### Villain Actions

Remion has three villain actions. He can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. He takes these actions in order and can use only one per round.
:
***Action 1: Affettuoso.*** Remion instructs his performers to sing in a tender and charming mood. *Charisma Saving Throw:* DC ??, all enemies within 30 feet of any of Remion's performers. *Failure:* The target is charmed by the closest performer to them. While charmed in this way, a creature can do nothing but use its movement to approach the performer in a safe manner. A charmed creature can repeat the Saving Throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. This effect also ends when Remion uses his second Villain Action.
:
***Action 2: Prestissimo.*** Remion instructs his performers to pick up the tempo, casting the *Haste* spell on each of them. These spells do not require concentration, and instead last for their full duration.
:
***Action 3: Fortissimo.*** Remion brings the performance to a crescendo, causing all attacks from his performers that hit to become Critical Hits. In addition, the frenzy causes all of Remion's allies to spend their turns moving in range and attacking the closest creature, ally or enemy.
